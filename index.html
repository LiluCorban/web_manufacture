ABOUT US

100% ECO PRODUCT

    At Kuhar'E , we carefully follow all the necessary conditions for the production of the correct rennet oil:

  • Manufacture room. Well ventilated, not raw, without direct sunlight with a temperature of up to 15 degrees, which allows to preserve all the qualities of raw materials
  • Quality of raw materials. The highest degree of purification (up to 99%). Nuts and seeds only salty
  • The heat treatment is completely excluded. Raw materials are not fried to enhance the taste qualities and as a consequence does not lose useful properties
  • Pressing oils exclusively with eco-equipment-hydraulic press with oak barrels
  • Pour oil immediately after spinning in dark glass bottles. To exclude world rays and as a result of oil oxidation
